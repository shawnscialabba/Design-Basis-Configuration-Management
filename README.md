# Design-Basis-Configuration-Management

### Summary

This repository details the protocols and processes utilized to manage rigorous Design Basis verification and Configuration Management. This ensures that the physical condition of the facility always matches the approved licensing basis documentation.

### Objectives

*   Spearhead rigorous Design Basis verification and Configuration Management protocols.
*   Mitigate enterprise risk associated with unanalyzed conditions or design changes.
*   Ensure 100% regulatory compliance across engineering modifications.

### Achievements & Impact

*   **Compliance:** Achieved **100% regulatory compliance** during audits of Design Basis and Configuration Management programs.
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Prevented an estimated **$70K+** in potential rework and re-submission costs by maintaining accurate documentation.
*   **Efficiency:** Drove initiatives that led to estimated annual cost savings of **$100k+** and elevated Quality Control metrics by **30%** (fewer non-conformities/NCRs).
*   **Governance:** Oversaw stringent DCD/ECN processes, managing 100+ engineering changes with zero non-conformances.
